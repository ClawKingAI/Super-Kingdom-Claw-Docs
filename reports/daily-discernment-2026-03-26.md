Passover is not history. The principle remains: when judgment passes through the land, only the blood of the Lamb protects. Israel's current war is physical, but the spiritual principle is identical. Churches should be teaching on the blood NOW — not as symbolism, but as spiritual reality.

**Application:** The Church should be "examining the Lamb" this week — fixing eyes on Yeshua, the Lamb of God who takes away the sin of the world. Not distracted by news cycles.

The Month of Nisan word is for NOW:

Passover was not just deliverance; it was judgment on Egypt's gods:

- Pharaoh (god-king) → his firstborn died

**Current Parallel:** Iran's regime claims divine authority. The attack on Israel (God's covenant people) near Passover may invite God's judgment on false gods.

**Scripture:** *"Against all the gods of Egypt I will execute judgment: I am the LORD."* (Exodus 12:12)

### 5. Elijah Is Coming (Shabbat HaGadol Haftarah)

**Application:** Before major divine intervention, God sends preparation. The current intensity may be part of that preparation — waking up the Church, warning Israel, exposing what is hidden.

### 6. The Fire Must Be Kept Burning (Parashat Tzav)

**Leviticus 6:13:** *"A fire shall always be burning on the altar; it shall never go out."*

**Application:** In times of war, the Church's fire must not go out. Prayer, worship, intercession — this is not the time to grow cold. The enemy wants distraction and fear. God says: keep the fire burning.
