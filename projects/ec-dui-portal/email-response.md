# Draft Email Response to EC DUI School Client

---

Hi [Client Name],

Thank you for the detailed overview — this gives me a clear picture of what you need. The EC DUI School Client Support Services Portal is absolutely something we can build.

Before I provide a formal quote, I have a few clarifying questions to ensure the scope and pricing are accurate:

---

**1. Referral Source Portal Access**

You mentioned probation officers will need to see their case members. A few follow-ups:
- Will probation officers log in themselves, or will you send them reports?
- Besides probation officers, will any other referral sources need portal access (courts, attorneys, DFACS)?
- Do you need them to just *view* records, or also upload documents and receive notifications?

---

**2. Program Tracking**

- Can a client be enrolled in multiple programs at once (e.g., FVIP + Anger Management simultaneously)?
- When a client completes a program, should they stay in the system for historical records, or be archived?

---

**3. Attendance Workflow**

- Do you record attendance daily, weekly, or per session?
- Who marks attendance — just you, or do instructors also need access?
- Should the system automatically flag clients approaching their 3-absence or 3-late limits, and if so, what action should be taken (notification, report, alert)?

---

**4. Payments & Integration**

You mentioned Stripe and QuickBooks integration via Bolt. To clarify:
- Should the portal automatically create payment records when Stripe payments come through, or will you also need manual payment entry (for cash, money orders, etc.)?
- Do you need the system to send payment reminders or invoices, or just record payments received?

---

**5. Document Management**

For the HushForms registration forms:
- Should the portal just *link* to the forms, or should submitted form data flow back into the portal automatically?
- If automatic, do you have API access with HushForms, or would a manual import/export workflow work for now?
- For the tutorial/orientation videos — do you have these already, or should I include video hosting/setup in the scope?

---

**6. Letter Generation**

- Do you have existing letterhead or templates for the Completion Letters, Termination Letters, and Transfer Letters that you can share?
- Should these be auto-generated as PDFs, printable directly from the portal, or emailed to recipients?

---

**7. Reporting & Forecasting**

You mentioned wanting statistics, analytics, and forecasting. To make sure I build what you actually need:
- What are the top 5 reports you'd use most? (e.g., monthly revenue by program, completion rates, attendance compliance, referral source activity)
- What kind of forecasting are you looking for? (e.g., projected revenue, expected completions, capacity planning)

---

**8. Timeline**

- Is there a specific deadline or compliance date you're working toward?
- What would constitute a successful Phase 1 launch for you? (e.g., "I can track clients, attendance, and payments" vs. "everything including integrations")

---

**9. Branding Assets**

- Do you have a logo file (PNG/SVG) and any official branding guidelines you can send?
- The color scheme you mentioned (blue, yellow, green, orange) — do you have specific hex codes, or should I match your existing materials?

---

**10. Letter of Completion Data**

You listed: start date, end date, total days attended, total absences, money paid, balance.
- Is there a standard format or template these letters must follow for court/agency acceptance?
- Do you need digital signatures on these letters?

---

Once I have your answers, I'll provide a detailed quote with phased delivery, timeline, and payment terms.

Looking forward to building this with you.
