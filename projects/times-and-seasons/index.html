A Teaching on Divine Timing

Times and Seasons

"The section from 12 to 3 is a quarter of the clock,
and the quarters together form the shape of a cross."

Understanding what should be taught and what scriptures should be used — not by repeating the same lessons, but by recognizing the lessons that apply to the specific season you are in.

Begin the Teaching

The Revelation

The Clock and the Cross

Immediately I heard the word quarters. Split the word up in quarterly pieces. The times and the seasons are a way to understand what should be taught and what scriptures should be used.

It does not always mean repeating the exact same lessons from scripture each time. Instead, there are lessons that apply individually to the specific season you are in.

I was shown a clock. The section from 12 to 3 is a quarter of the clock, and the quarters together form the shape of a cross. It represents the times and the seasons and how they divide and mark different moments.

There are many layers of meaning in the times and seasons and in the things taught in the Word. Some messages are straightforward, but they apply for different reasons depending on the season.

The message is to bring awareness to the Word and to the times and seasons. Many of the things God is trying to show His children are missed because people are not willing to go deeper into the scriptures that relate to the times and seasons.

Discernment

Why This Teaching Matters Now

Revelation is important right now. Attention needs to be brought to the things happening and how they align with biblical scripture. This also includes prophecy and what the book of Revelation teaches about prophecy and discernment.

Many people are not paying attention to these things. Instead, they focus more on what is being spoken through media rather than learning how to discern truth from falsehood.

The purpose is to bring awareness and instruction about how events align with the times and seasons described in scripture. People today pay much attention to their phones, electronics, and social media, but these things often distract them from the truth they are seeking.

Many spend days worrying about things that are not aligned with what is actually happening in the times and seasons. The call is for people to hear the Word and learn how to discern the truth and recognize how it aligns with scripture.

When this happens, it draws people closer to hearing God's voice.

Encouragement

For the Teacher and Hearers

Do not be discouraged by meetings that are small or large. As you continue to do His will, there will be more. There is a short period of time to bring people back, to turn their hearts back toward God, to renew and restore them to who they were created to be.

Many no longer know Him. Messages that reveal truth will bring comfort, even when the truth is difficult to hear, because the Word of God is truth.

Guiding people through this time is important. This is a season of alignment and preparation. There is a renewing happening in the land and in the places that are called for it. More will be revealed as time goes on, but the message now is to begin.

This is a season where people are ready to receive. Many are searching for hope because fear, confusion, and discouragement have been spread. When truth is spoken and hope is given, it becomes the light of the world and the salt of the earth, which is what believers are called to be.

As this work continues, more anointing will come. One step in faith will lead to another. Trust and speak. God will provide the words and lead the way.

Trust begins small, but as you step forward it grows stronger. Rest in Him and trust Him. There is a deeper connection that will come while teaching, like receiving understanding directly from heaven. As you step forward, things will make more sense and you will begin to see from a higher perspective.

This is a season of renewal—renewing faith and awakening a fresh fire, like encountering God again for the first time. Each step of growth brings a deeper connection. Often the bridge into that deeper place is simply trust and faith.

This week you can begin leading people to understand what is happening in the world and how it aligns with scripture and the times and seasons. People are reaching a point where they are ready for truth. Many beliefs are being shaken so that truth can be received.

Identity

Identity, Warfare, and Alignment

Continue praying for people. There should also be provision for offerings or donations for outreach and ministry work. As time goes on, the reason for this provision will become clearer. God will provide for His children. Do not be afraid to ask for support when it is for the purpose of spreading the Word and helping others. This itself is a teaching that will become important in the right season.

The enemy attempts to distract, discourage, and distort thinking in order to block the work being done. He tries to provoke reactions that create openings for lies or discouragement. Resist these thoughts. Speak truth over them.

When lies are believed, they interrupt the flow of God's Spirit. Speaking truth restores that flow and pushes the enemy away. When weariness or disturbance appears, speak the truth of God's Word. This restores the path forward.

Be strong and courageous. Build your life on God's promises. Sometimes resistance comes from patterns formed in the past, but the old person is gone. A new life has begun. Expect good things. Expect new things. Expect love, favor, and blessing.

Identity in Christ is part of the armor believers carry. Knowing who you are in God is essential. Move forward in faith without fear.

Remember daily how loved and valued you are by God, not by the opinions of the world. Worldly circumstances often trap people because they seek validation from others rather than looking to God. When alignment returns, God places people in positions of favor and surrounds them with people who bless them.

Believing in God also means believing in what He has placed within you. God's Spirit clears away confusion, doubt, and residue from the world. His presence renews and lifts His people.

There are biblical patterns in the times and seasons that bring comfort and understanding. God does not waste any part of a person's journey. Even challenges build character. Mountains may be placed before you, but God provides the strength and tools needed to climb them. At the summit there are treasures and breakthroughs prepared.

Hold closely the truth of who God says you are. Neither of you arrived here by accident. This path was prepared. Continue trusting Him and walking forward each day renewed.

Divine Timing

The Month of Nissan

A Season of Piercing, Healing, and Divine Realignment

The Piercing

There has been a piercing. A place of infection that needed to be drained. Sometimes there's experiences that my children go through that feel painful. Or can seem pointless or dreadful. Or feel separated from my kingdom or from me.

Part of the journey is to bring my kingdom to earth, and to others who haven't experienced or don't know me.

There has been what seems like a wall. That the enemy has placed multiple layers of walls to cause more of an issue for my children to see through, over or see another way instead of just plowing through the walls and taking a harder route.

The enemy provides a false image of what the path or the exit or your pathway looks like to frustrate and to corner you into taking the wrong turn. Especially for my anointed chosen ones, he wants to create frustration or to take you down a path that just takes longer to get to where you're going. If he can't keep you away, he will just try to keep you delayed.

But my children, the piercing has happened. And I have laid down judgment. And it is in process from the heavens to earth. That I am fighting. I am fighting for you.

Fresh Manna and New Strength

This month is about regaining strength for gaining higher knowledge. The strength is mental and spiritual, but the spirit strengthens the body. It needs to be fed with fresh manna and fresh fire.

Some of my children have been feeding off stale and old bread that no longer has nutrients in it the way it did before. And some see and hear of this and know there's another way or know there are things waiting, but they can't see over the wall and over what the enemy has placed in front of them to move anyways.

And this is because before they move, they must have this inner strength and feed and feast off of the fresh manna baked with a fresh fire from the heavens, a pure unleavened manna. It's from me, straight from me.

Some of my children have fed too much of their mind and their own understanding, and it blocks them from me and for me feeding them with the new things that I have for them.

To seek my face is not always to seek understanding. It's to seek me and to wait and to worship and praise and to love me. To expect me to do what I say that I will do.

The Path Already Paved

There's already a path paved. There's already a way that has been set, and there isn't any disruptions on this path with me if you stay focused on me.

Some of my children live a life on guessing or just a wheel of fortune instead of sitting with me and allowing me to steer. They have a misunderstanding of the way my kingdom works.

I love all of my children, and I want them to love each other. There's so many people in the world that are distracted and disturbed and disrupted by comparison or seeing what others have instead of seeking me to let me show them how much I love them and to show them what I have specifically, personally written on their life for them.

There is no one better or worse. Everyone is unique and everyone has a purpose that needs to be fulfilled. Each part matters, and each part, there are different parts of my children's lives that have assignments, and those assignments, each one is important to the next and to whoever you encounter.

Healing and Renewal

This is a month of healing as well, but healing comes in many forms. This can be healing the heart, healing the renewal of your mind, healing the joy in your soul, healing the excitement of the new things, renewing and giving a fresh healing of excitement and drive.

And this is also a physical healing. Many need to be healed in their movement. So it's also a month of shaking to awaken where my sun begins to shine brighter over my children.

This is also a way of refueling and reviving to bring out more and new growth, to shine light on things that are no longer needed, and this can be habits, thought processes, friendships, careers, family members, relationships that don't work. This also can be as simple as your diet or the routine that you have needs to be changed.

And part of the process with growth is resting, and my children need to rest in me. Allow me to bring healing. Allow me to renew your mind. Allow me to explain my ways in the ways of your heart so I can heal this. Turn to me and rest in me so I can heal.

Intimacy with God

He's showing me an image of Jesus standing at the veil, like a man frustrated leaning against a wall, and Jesus' hand is on the other side of the veil holding him, reaching out while the man is in frustration.

This is a month of intimacy with me.

He's showing me that angels are singing and there's something in the heavens, something special, like they're ready, like there's a shift happening in this month. There's a lifting taking place right now, a release. There are so many breaking free.

So don't hesitate to sit in prayer and ask me for the things that you need so that I can help place you in the direction you need to go to make things as smooth as possible for you.

Staying in prayer and praise and worship and speaking to me is going to move things along faster and to command the things that you need for my angels to assist. There's so many things that happen in a day in one person's life that they could have more understanding or have a smoother day if they just started it by sitting with me and speaking to me and asking and taking dominion before the enemy has a chance to speak.

Healing and Fasting

This month is a month to lay hands on the sick. There's more blessing on healing and it is faster, it comes quicker right now. The timing will be sped up this month on healing.

The healing can be miracle because of the time it was taking for someone to be healed. And sometimes when my children need to be healed, they need to fast to reset things in the spirit and in the physical. This literally can move time on a cellular level.

This is why it's difficult for my children sometimes to fast because of the progress and breakthrough they receive from doing it. It's a key. It's like building muscle, it has to be broken down to be built back up.

This is the same thing for this month. There's a cleansing, a refreshing renewal. Sometimes you have to open the wound to drain it, to clean it, to bring in new healing, the new skin, the new wineskin.

So prepare and expect a shift and a renewal of things. This is not just a normal time of year. This year specifically it is different, and this month has a more pronounced timing in it because of alignment. It goes deeper than most know.

Walk in This Word

Take these words and sit with me, allow me to heal, to give revelation, to shine my light on the things I'm trying to show you, and allow me to heal you and bandage you and give you the new things that you were supposed to already have.

But the world enters and infects things so it has to be extracted and cleaned properly. Sometimes my children don't want to face the piercing because it seems painful, but in order for this to heal, this must happen. And I can renew completely.

This month is a time for it to fully happen at supernatural speed.

So bless somebody else with kind words, and bless yourselves with kind words. Lay hands on the sick, the broken-hearted, and the lost. My love is the greatest healing ointment. It's received through me, but I also place it in you to give to others so that you can be my hands and feet.

Knowing how much I love you makes this easier. So first know who you are in me and how much you're loved, and sit with me so I can show you this.

In Jesus' name.

Response

Reflection and Response

Reflection Questions

  • 1. What season do you believe you are currently in spiritually? How does this affect what God might be speaking to you?
  • 2. Where have you been seeking understanding instead of seeking God's face? What would it look like to simply wait and worship?
  • 3. What "walls" has the enemy placed before you? Are you being delayed or redirected?
  • 4. What stale bread have you been feeding on? What fresh manna is God offering you this season?
  • 5. Who can you bless with kind words this week? Who can you pray for or lay hands on?

Prayer Focus This Week

Daily: Begin each day sitting with God, speaking to Him, and taking dominion before the enemy has a chance to speak.
Scripture: Go deeper into the scriptures that relate to times and seasons. Ask God to show you what applies to this moment.
Healing: Ask God to show you areas needing piercing and healing. Trust Him with the process.
Fasting: Consider if God is calling you to fast for breakthrough—spiritually, mentally, or physically.

Action Steps

Identify one area where you've been feeding on "stale bread" and replace it with fresh time in God's presence.
Write down three lies the enemy has been speaking and speak God's truth over each one.
Pray for someone this week who needs healing—physical, emotional, or spiritual.
Share this teaching with someone who needs to hear about God's timing and seasons.
