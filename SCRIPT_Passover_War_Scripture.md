# VIDEO SCRIPT: PASSOVER, WAR, AND THE APPOINTED TIMES

**Length:** ~15-20 minutes
**Format:** Teleprompter-ready
**Date:** Saturday, March 28, 2026 / 10 Nisan 5786

---

## 🎬 OPENING HOOK

**(Direct to camera, strong eye contact)**

Today is 10 Nisan.

On THIS exact day, 3,500 years ago, the Israelites in Egypt selected their Passover lamb.

For four days, that lamb would be examined.

Watched.

Tested.

And on the fourth day — it would be sacrificed.

**(Pause)**

Brothers and sisters, we are IN that four-day window RIGHT NOW.

And God is examining His Church.

**(2-3 second beat)**

---

## 📅 SECTION 1: THE APPOINTED TIME

**(Gesture toward calendar/date on screen if available)**

Let me show you something.

**(Slightly slower, emphatic)**

Today is 10 Nisan.

The Hebrew month of Nisan is called **Rosh Chodashim** — the HEAD of months.

God didn't start His calendar with creation.

He started it with REDEMPTION.

**(Pause for impact)**

The word "Nisan" comes from the Hebrew word **NAS** — meaning "miracle."

And **NITZAN** — meaning "bud" or "blossom."

This is the month of SPRING.

Of NEW BEGINNINGS.

Of MIRACLES.

**(Beat)**

But here's what you need to understand:

This Sabbath is called **SHABBAT HAGADOL** — "The Great Sabbath."

Why?

Because on THIS Sabbath, in Egypt, the Israelites took the Passover lamb.

And the Egyptians COULDN'T STOP THEM.

**(Strong)**

God was showing them: I WILL PROTECT MY PEOPLE.

---

## 📖 SECTION 2: THE PERPETUAL FIRE

**(Open Bible gesture)**

The Torah portion for this week is **Parashat Tzav** — Leviticus 6 through 8.

And there's a verse that BURNS off the page in this hour.

**(Slow, emphatic reading)**

**Leviticus 6, verse 6:**

> "A perpetual fire shall always be burning on the altar; it shall never go out."

**(Pause)**

The Hebrew word is **TAMID** — "continually."

This fire was NEVER supposed to go out.

Day or night.

Sabbath or feast.

War or peace.

**(Strong, building intensity)**

In a time of GLOBAL WAR, God is saying to His Church:

**KEEP THE FIRE BURNING.**

Prayer.

Worship.

Intercession.

This is not the hour to let the altar go cold.

---

## 🌍 SECTION 3: THE WAR AND THE WORD

**(Transition tone — serious, weighty)**

Now I want to show you something that will shake you.

**(Slight pause)**

The war that is raging right now — in Israel, Lebanon, Yemen, Iran — is NOT random.

It was WRITTEN.

**(Build intensity gradually)**

### The Houthi Attack

This week, Yemen's Houthis launched a missile at Israel for the FIRST TIME since this war began.

**(Pause for impact)**

The media calls it an "escalation."

The Bible calls it by name.

**(Open Bible gesture)**

**Psalm 83, verses 4 through 8:**

> "They have said, Come, and let us cut them off from being a nation... The tabernacles of EDOM, and the ISHMAELITES; of MOAB, and the HAGARENES; GEBAL, and AMMON, and AMALEK; the PHILISTINES with the inhabitants of TYRE; ASSUR also is joined with them..."

**(Strong)**

Let me show you what this means TODAY:

**(Use hand gestures to map if helpful)**

- **Edom** — Jordan, Palestinian territories
- **Ishmaelites / Hagarenes** — Arab nations, including YEMEN
- **Moab and Ammon** — Modern Jordan
- **Philistines** — Gaza, Hamas
- **Tyre** — Southern Lebanon, HEZBOLLAH
- **Assur** — Syria, Iraq... and now IRAN

**(Beat)**

The Bible NAMED this confederacy 3,000 years ago.

And it's standing against Israel RIGHT NOW.

---

## ⚔️ SECTION 4: ELAM AND PERSIA

**(Transition — prophetic weight)**

There's more.

**(Pause)**

This month, Israel struck Iranian nuclear facilities.

Uranium plants. Steel factories. Heavy water complexes.

The IRGC Navy chief was killed.

**(Slow, emphatic)**

The media reports the news.

The Bible reports WHAT IT MEANS.

**(Open Bible gesture)**

**Jeremiah 49, verses 34 through 39:**

> "The word of the LORD that came to Jeremiah the prophet against ELAM... For I will cause to cease in Elam the SUBSTANCE of the kingdom... But it shall come to pass in the LATTER DAYS, that I will bring again the captivity of Elam, saith the LORD."

**(Strong)**

Elam is the ancient name for WESTERN IRAN.

The Hebrew word for "substance" — **QEREN** — means "horn, strength, power."

God said: In the latter days, I will BREAK the power of Elam.

**(Beat)**

And then — restoration.

**(Pause)**

Israel's strikes may be part of this prophetic sequence.

---

## 💰 SECTION 5: ECONOMIC WARFARE

**(Transition — economic focus)**

But it's not just missiles.

Iran has now established what they're calling a "toll booth" on the Strait of Hormuz.

**(Gesture: choke point)**

Twenty percent of the world's oil flows through that strait.

And Iran is HOLDING IT HOSTAGE.

**(Open Bible gesture)**

**Revelation 18, verse 11:**

> "And the merchants of the earth shall weep and mourn over her; for no man buyeth their merchandise any more..."

**(Pause)**

The Greek word for "merchants" is **EMPOROI** — wholesalers, those who trade in BULK.

And verse 17:

> "For in ONE HOUR so great riches is come to nought."

**(Strong)**

**MIA HORA** — "ONE HOUR."

Sudden. Catastrophic. GLOBAL.

**(Beat)**

The infrastructure for the collapse of Babylon's economic system is being built RIGHT NOW.

---

## 🤖 SECTION 6: DECEPTION AND DELUSION

**(Transition — slower, more serious)**

But there's something else.

**(Pause)**

This week, a landmark court case found social media companies LIABLE for addiction.

A new study warns that AI is "giving bad advice to flatter its users."

**(Slow, emphatic)**

We are watching the construction of a GLOBAL DECEPTION MACHINE.

**(Open Bible gesture)**

**2 Thessalonians 2, verses 10 and 11:**

> "...because they received not the love of the truth... God shall send them STRONG DELUSION, that they should believe a lie."

**(Strong)**

The Greek word for "strong delusion" is **ENERGEIA PLANES** — "energized deception."

God doesn't just ALLOW deception.

He ENERGIZES it — as JUDGMENT on those who reject truth.

**(Pause)**

**Revelation 13:**

> "And he had power to give LIFE to the image of the beast, that the image should SPEAK."

**(Look directly at camera)**

AI can make images SPEAK now.

Deepfakes. Chatbots. Synthetic voices.

**(Beat)**

The technology for Revelation 13 EXISTS.

---

## ❤️ SECTION 7: THE REFINER'S FIRE

**(Transition — pastoral, warm but serious)**

Brothers and sisters...

**(Pause, soften tone)**

The Haftarah for this Shabbat HaGadol is from **Malachi 3.**

**(Open Bible gesture)**

**Malachi 3, verses 2 and 3:**

> "But who may abide the day of his coming? ... for he is like a REFINER'S FIRE, and like fullers' soap: And he shall sit as a refiner and purifier of silver..."

**(Gentle but firm)**

The Hebrew word **TZEREF** — "refiner."

The refiner SITS by the fire.

He WATCHES.

He knows exactly how long to leave the silver in the flame.

**(Build intensity)**

The war.

The shaking.
The testing.

**(Strong)**

This is the REFINER'S FIRE.

And God is asking:

**WHO WILL STAND?**

**(2-3 second beat)**

---

## 🔥 SECTION 8: THE FOUR-DAY EXAMINATION

**(Transition — clear, direct application)**

Remember:

The lamb was selected on 10 Nisan.

For FOUR DAYS, it was examined.

Watched.

Tested.

**(Look directly at camera, strong eye contact)**

**Yeshua was examined for four days.**

By the Pharisees.

By Herod.
By Pilate.

**(Build)**

And NOW — in THIS four-day window before Passover — God is examining US.

Testing our faith.

Our love.
Our obedience.

**(Strong)**

The question is not: Will there be a test?

The question is: **WILL YOU PASS?**

## 📢 SECTION 9: WHAT MUST BE DONE

**(Transition — call to action)**

So what do we DO?

**(Clear, direct)**

### 1. KEEP THE FIRE BURNING

Prayer. Worship. Intercession.

Don't let the altar go cold.

### 2. STAND WITH ISRAEL

Pray for Jerusalem (Psalm 122:6).

Understand God's covenant (Romans 11).

### 3. PREPARE

Spiritually. Physically. Emotionally.

Joseph stored grain BEFORE the famine.

**(Strong)**

Preparation is not fear.

Preparation is WISDOM.

### 4. DISCERN THE TIMES

Don't be deceived.

Test everything by the Word of God.

**(Beat)**

### 5. TELL OTHERS

Your family needs to know.

Your church needs to know.

**(Strong)**

**The appointed times are not arbitrary.**

**God's calendar matters.**
