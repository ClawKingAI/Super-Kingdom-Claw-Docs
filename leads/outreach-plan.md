# Five-Fold Webinar Outreach Plan

## Target Audience
People who are:
- Hungry for Kingdom restoration
- Questioning traditional church structure
- Already meeting in house churches or home groups
- Interested in Five-Fold Ministry (apostles, prophets, evangelists, pastors, teachers)
- Seeking spiritual gifts and Kingdom power
- Familiar with ekklesia and organic church concepts
