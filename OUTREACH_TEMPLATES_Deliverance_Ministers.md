## BEST PRACTICES FOR OUTREACH

1. **Personalize:** Always use their name if you have it
2. **Be brief:** Ministry leaders are busy — get to the point
3. **Offer value first:** Don't just pitch — show how this serves THEM
4. **Honor their work:** Acknowledge what they're doing for the Kingdom
5. **No pressure:** Give them an easy out if they're not interested
6. **Follow up once:** One follow-up is enough — don't chase
