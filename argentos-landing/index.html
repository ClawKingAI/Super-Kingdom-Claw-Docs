Core systems designed for autonomous operation, not just response generation

🧠

Hybrid Memory

SQLite + pgvector hybrid search with entity tracking. Your AI remembers conversations, relationships, and context across sessions.

💓

Heartbeat System

Contract and promise system with periodic accountability. Your AI commits to tasks and reports back — no more forgotten promises.

🔄

Contemplation Loop

Always-on thinking powered by local Qwen via Ollama. Your AI processes, reflects, and prepares even when you're not prompting.

Visual Workflows

Multi-agent pipeline builder with 62 connectors. Design complex automations visually without writing code.

🎯

Intent System

3-tier hierarchical policy engine ensures your AI acts within boundaries you define. Global, agent, and task-level policies.

🔀

Model Router

Complexity-based routing between local/fast/balanced/powerful models. Use the right intelligence for each task.

📈

Self-Improving System

SIS extracts lessons from every interaction, continuously improving behavior and decision-making over time.

React Dashboard

WebGL avatar, chat interface, and task board in a beautiful dashboard. Monitor and interact with your AI visually.

Knowledge Library

RAG-powered knowledge base with agent-level ACL. Share information selectively across your AI agents.
