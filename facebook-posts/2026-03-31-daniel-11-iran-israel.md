Israel has dropped **13,000 bombs** on Iran in the past month.

Why does this matter?

---

Daniel 11:43 says:

*"He shall have power over the treasures of gold and silver, and over all the precious things of Egypt."*

The prophecy describes an end-times king who doesn't just destroy armies — he **controls resources**.

Israel is now doing exactly that: targeting Iran's ability to fund its war machine.

---

But here's what's wild:

The US has set an **April 6 deadline** for Iran to surrender.

That deadline falls during **Passover week**.

The feast commemorating Israel's deliverance from bondage... while Israel is at war with Persia (Iran) again.

History is repeating.

---

Daniel 11:40-45 describes:
- A sweeping military campaign ("like a whirlwind")
- An invasion of Israel ("enter the Glorious Land")
- Control of economic resources ("treasures of gold and silver")
- News from the east and north that "troubles him"
- A headquarters planted "between the seas and the glorious holy mountain" — Jerusalem
- Sudden destruction with "no one to help him"

---

We may not be in the Tribulation yet.

But we are watching the stage being set.

And the convergence of Israel vs. Iran during Passover week is not a coincidence.
