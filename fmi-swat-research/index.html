Legal Definition: The terrestrial realm ordained to respond to the voice of man. As God spoke creation into being, man — made in God's image — carries delegated authority to speak to earth.

Scriptural Function

The Battle of Altars

In spiritual warfare, altars represent covenantal agreements — both godly and ungodly. The believer's authority includes confronting evil altars and establishing righteous ones.

Ungodly Altars

Covenant agreements with darkness that grant legal ground to the enemy. Must be identified, renounced, and destroyed.

Godly Altars

Covenant agreements established with God that release His power and presence. To be maintained and activated through prayer and decree.

Tactical Boundaries

Guardrails for safe and effective spiritual warfare

✓ Authorized Operations

  • • Speaking to creation within your God-assigned jurisdiction
  • • Decreeing Scripture-based promises over your sphere
  • • Commanding demonic entities to release what belongs to you
  • • Addressing natural elements in alignment with God's will
  • • Enforcing verdicts received in heavenly court

✗ Unauthorized Operations

  • • Operating outside your assigned jurisdiction without commission
  • • Speaking presumption instead of Rhema (revealed Word)
  • • Engaging territorial spirits over regions not assigned to you
  • • Using spiritual authority for personal vengeance or gain
  • • Decreeing anything contrary to the nature and will of God
