Bottom Line

GLM-5 is 90-95% as capable as Claude Opus 4.6 and GPT-5.4 — but costs 15-25x less.

For most practical work (coding, analysis, research), the gap is barely noticeable. Kimi K2.5 even ties Opus on competition math.

Use Claude/GPT when you need maximum capability and cost doesn't matter. Use GLM-5/Kimi when you want nearly the same performance at a fraction of the price.
