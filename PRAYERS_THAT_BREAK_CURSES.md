## SECTION 1: FOUNDATIONAL PRAYERS

### 1. Prayer to Enter the Courts of Heaven

*Use this before any specific curse-breaking prayer.*

---

**Father in Heaven,**

I come before Your Courts today, not on my own merit, but by the blood of Yeshua, my High Priest after the order of Melchizedek.

I approach You as Judge — the Judge of all the earth who does what is right.

I ask that my case be heard today.

Let my accuser be silenced.

Let the records of heaven be opened.

Let every legal right the enemy has claimed over my life be exposed and annulled.

I present myself before Your throne, asking for justice, for release, and for the restoration of everything stolen from me.

In the name of Yeshua, my Advocate, I pray.

**Amen.**

### 2. Prayer of Repentance and Legal Cleansing

*Use this to clear yourself before God so the enemy has no legal ground.*

---

**Father,**

I repent of all sin — known and unknown.

I confess: I have sinned against You in thought, word, and deed.

I have broken Your commandments.

I have allowed footholds for the enemy through disobedience, unforgiveness, fear, and compromise.

**I repent now.**

I turn from every sin.

I renounce every agreement with darkness.

I cancel every word, covenant, or promise I made — knowingly or unknowingly — with demonic powers.

I apply the blood of Yeshua over every sin, every transgression, every iniquity.

Let the blood speak better things than the blood of Abel (Hebrews 12:24).

Let the blood silence every accusation.

Let the blood cleanse my conscience from dead works.

**Wash me, Lord. Make me clean.**

I ask for a verdict of "NOT GUILTY" in the Courts of Heaven.

In Yeshua's name.

**Amen.**

**I repent now on behalf of my bloodline.**

I stand in the gap for my father's house and my mother's house.

I confess these sins before You, Judge of all the earth.

I ask: **Let the record show these sins are forgiven by the blood of Yeshua.**

I renounce every ancestral altar.

I renounce every covenant made with demons by my ancestors.

I renounce every dedication — whether of myself, my siblings, my parents, or my grandparents — to false gods, marine spirits, or occult powers.

**I uproot every ancestral altar now.**

By the authority of Yeshua, I command:

- Let every generational curse be BROKEN.
- Let every ancestral covenant be ANNULLED.
- Let every demonic altar be UPROOTED.
- Let every legal right claimed by the enemy be REVOKED.

I declare: **I am FREE from the sins of my ancestors.**

I establish a new altar — the altar of Yeshua — over my life and my bloodline.

From this day forward, I and my house will serve the LORD.

In Yeshua's name.

**Amen.**
