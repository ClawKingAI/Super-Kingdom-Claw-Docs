FAQs

How does the AI-powered design process work?

Our AI analyzes your requirements and generates initial design concepts. Human designers then refine and perfect these concepts to ensure uniqueness and brand alignment.

What technologies do you use?

We build with React, Next.js, and TailwindCSS for modern, performant sites. We also offer static HTML exports for maximum compatibility.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery ranges from 48 hours (Quick Launch) to 1 week (Professional). Rush delivery available for additional fee.

Do you offer hosting?

Yes! All plans include deployment via here.now with instant live URLs. Higher tiers include custom domain setup.

What if I need revisions?

Each tier includes revision rounds (1-3 depending on plan). Additional revisions can be purchased. We work closely with you to get it right.
