## USAGE NOTES

**Best Practices:**
1. Always personalize the bracketed [fields] - generic sends get flagged as spam
2. Test subject lines with small batches (20-50) before scaling
3. Follow CAN-SPAM rules: include unsubscribe option for emails
4. Track response rates by template to identify top performers
5. A/B test tone (casual vs. direct) based on audience

**Platform Guidelines:**
- Twitter/X: Keep under 280 characters; use threaded replies for longer messages
- LinkedIn: More formal tone acceptable; InMail has character limits
- Instagram: Visual-first; consider mock-up preview in first message
- Email: Subject lines under 50 characters for mobile

**Response Templates:**

When they reply interested:
> "Great to hear! Let's jump on a 15-min call to discuss what you need. Here's my calendar: [link]. Or if you prefer, send me a bit about your business and I'll put together a quick mock-up concept."

When they ask about pricing:
> "I keep it simple: $500 for a quick launch (3 days), $1,500 for a full business presence with SEO (1 week), or $3,000 for custom builds with integrations (2 weeks). Depends on where you're at and what you need. Want to hop on a call and figure out which makes sense?"

When they say "not interested":
> "No worries at all. If anything changes down the road, I'm here. Best of luck with [business name]!"
